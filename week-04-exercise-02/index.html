The Beauty Edit

I'm really into makeup, so I decided to make my collection about some of my favorite beauty products. I enjoy trying new products and finding ones that work well for everyday makeup looks.

Benefit Highlighter

Brand: Benefit | Category: Highlighter

I like this highlighter because it gives my skin a natural glow without looking too sparkly. It's great for everyday makeup.

Charlotte Tilbury Setting Spray

Brand: Charlotte Tilbury | Category: Setting Spray

This setting spray helps my makeup last longer and keeps it looking fresh throughout the day.

Huda Beauty Powder

Brand: Huda Beauty | Category: Powder

I like this powder because it helps reduce shine and leaves my makeup looking smooth and soft.

Make Up For Ever Lip Product

Brand: Make Up For Ever | Category: Lip Product

This lip product has a soft formula and adds a nice touch of color that works with almost any makeup look.

Rhode Blush

Brand: Rhode | Category: Blush

I like this blush because it has a creamy formula that blends easily and gives a soft, natural flush.

Saie Glow Product

Brand: Saie | Category: Glow Product

This product gives my skin a dewy finish and makes my makeup look more fresh and radiant.
